About me

Since 14 years of age, I took a significant interest in software development. This interest arose when I noticed websites and android applications acquiring more sophistication somewhere in the range of 2011 and 2012. From that second I set out to develop something similar by concentrating, working hard and listening attentively to my superiors. Now the year is 2020, and have accomplished the objectives I set out. Recently, I took a full-stack developer course and went through intensive learning. The result from it was exceptional. I turned into a certified web developer, with knowledge in HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, PHP and React.js in a short space of time.

To reinforce my passion for software development, in late 2019 I graduated in Computer Science, earning a 2:1 bachelor honours. From this experience, I became familiar fundamentally in Java and Python. Not only did my university experience help develop the use of algorithmic thinking, it helped me to fully comprehend the structure of coding. This is invaluable knowledge, as it implies I can tackle any problems, regardless of the programming language. In my final year I became particularly fond of Java as it helped me frame an understanding in Android Studio, where I've created a multitude of android apps and have distributed in the Google Play Store.

Outside my hobbies in computing, I enjoy socialising with my friends and family, watching TV, staying active, driving long distances and playing video games from time to time.
